About

Charlie Chen, MD
Plastic Surgery

Dr. Charlie Chen is a premier, board-certified plastic and reconstructive surgeon with a distinguished Ivy League pedigree. A former Manhattan plastic surgeon, Dr. Chen completed his general surgery residency at Yale University and his plastic surgery residency at UC San Diego, followed by an ASAPS-endorsed aesthetic surgery fellowship in Charlotte, NC.

Now bi-coastal — serving both San Diego and Chicago — Dr. Chen brings cutting-edge techniques to a warm, boutique-style practice. As a fourth-generation physician trained in both plastic and general surgery, he combines deep medical expertise with an artistic eye for natural, elegant results.

His signature Preservé breast augmentation technique preserves natural tissue, ligaments, and nerves for less pain, preserved sensation, and faster recovery.

Signature Technique

Preservé Breast Augmentation

Dr. Chen's signature Preservé technique represents a paradigm shift in breast augmentation — delivering beautiful, lasting results with a significantly reduced recovery burden and preserved natural feeling.

  • Minimally invasive approach with smaller incisions
  • Preserves natural breast tissue, ligaments & nerves
  • Significantly less post-operative pain
  • Maintained nipple sensation
  • Faster recovery — return to life sooner
  • Natural, beautiful results that move and feel real
